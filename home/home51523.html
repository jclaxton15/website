Ransomware that encrypts itself





Here are 10 ways to fight off a cyber attack:
Prioritize cyber attack at your organization!
Next, you'll want to patch vulnerabilities.
Train your team and allow your team to grow and diversify.
Security awareness training is always good.
Update devices that connect to the network.
Make passwords just as important as anything else with requirements.
Backups, backups, backups.
Avoid using unsecure networks.
Also, encrypt your data!
