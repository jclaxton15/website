Ransomware that encrypts itself





There's a new variant of ransomware with the ability to evade antivirus by encrypting itself.
This ransomware strand is called Cactus.
How it works?
This variant will encrypt itself and has different modes of encryption which includes quick and can be ran twice outputting more than one file extension.
